Join us for a day on the green with purpose! The Georgia Public Defense Foundation Golf Tournament is more than just a game—it’s an opportunity to support justice, education, and second chances.

Your participation and generosity will help fund vital training for public defenders and reentry programs for individuals transitioning out of the criminal justice system, ensuring they have the resources needed to rebuild their lives.

Tee off for a cause and be part of the change. Let’s make a lasting impact—together!
