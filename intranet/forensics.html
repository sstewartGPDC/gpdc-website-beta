Forensic Resources

Learn to effectively challenge forensic evidence in criminal cases.

Forensic evidence is seemingly some of the strongest evidence brought forth in a courtroom. The problem is that most attorneys, judges, and jurors do not understand the science behind this evidence. Much of this evidence is rife with errors and bad science.

With these forensic disciplines proliferating in both the courtroom and popular culture, it is not enough anymore for an attorney to simply take the expert's report at face value. We must learn how to discuss, utilize, and litigate this evidence effectively.
