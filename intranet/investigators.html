About SWIG

The Statewide Investigators Group (SWIG) is a collaborative network of public defender investigators across Georgia. SWIG provides shared resources, training opportunities, and a platform for investigators to connect and share best practices.

  • Shared investigation resources and templates
  • Statewide investigator directory
  • JCATS training and support
  • Quarterly meetings and trainings
