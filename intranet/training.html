Transition into Law Practice Program (TILPP)

The TILPP is the mentoring and continuing legal education requirement for most lawyers newly admitted to the State Bar of Georgia. GPDC is authorized to conduct its own TILPP specifically for public defenders.

1 Fundamentals of Public Defense

  • Professionalism and the Role of the Public Defender
  • Ethics and Client Relations
  • Theory of the Case
  • Client Interviews
  • Bond Hearings and Preliminary Hearings
  • Discovery, Brady, and Investigations
  • Motions Practice
  • Sentencing and Plea Negotiations
  • Mental Health Issues
  • Probation Revocations

2 Trial Skills

  • Theory of the Case and Storytelling
  • Jury Selection
  • Opening Statements
  • Cross-Examination
  • Impeachment and Working with Documents
  • Direct Examination
  • Closing Arguments
