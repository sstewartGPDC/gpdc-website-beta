Mental Health Advocacy Resources

Resources and support from the Office of the Mental Health Advocate (OMHA) for representing clients with mental illness.

About the Office of the Mental Health Advocate

The Office of the Mental Health Advocate (OMHA) provides specialized support for public defenders representing clients with mental health conditions. Our team is available to assist with case consultation, resource identification, and training on mental health-related legal issues.

For case consultation or questions, contact OMHA at omha@gapubdef.org
