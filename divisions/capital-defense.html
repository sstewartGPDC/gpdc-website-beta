ABOUT THE DIVISION

Expert Capital Representation

Our Mission

The Capital Defense Division provides expert representation in death penalty cases through specialized defense teams. Our attorneys are among the most experienced capital litigators in Georgia, bringing exceptional skill and dedication to every case.

Our Approach

Capital cases require the highest level of legal expertise and resources. Each case is supported by a comprehensive team including two death penalty-qualified attorneys, a mitigation specialist, and a fact investigator, ensuring that every aspect of the defense is thoroughly addressed.
