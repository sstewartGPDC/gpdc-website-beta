ABOUT THE DIVISION

Dedicated Youth Advocacy

Our Mission

The Youth Advocacy Division (YAD) is dedicated to improving outcomes for youth exposed to the criminal justice system. We provide immediate, skilled, and dedicated legal representation to young people across Georgia.

Our Approach

Our approach addresses resource disparities in under-resourced counties to ensure quality defense access for all youth. We recruit qualified youth defenders and provide statewide training and support programs, while establishing constructive partnerships to enhance defense capabilities.

OUR SERVICES

What We Provide

Comprehensive youth defense services focused on rehabilitation and future success.

Direct Client Representation

Providing dedicated legal advocacy to indigent youth throughout the criminal justice process, ensuring their rights are protected.

Legal Practice & Advocacy

Comprehensive legal work across justice system levels to secure fair outcomes for young clients through strategic defense.

Equity Focus

Addressing resource disparities in under-resourced counties to ensure quality defense access for all youth across Georgia.
