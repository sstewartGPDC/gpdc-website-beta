ABOUT THE DIVISION

Specialized Mental Health Advocacy

Our Mission

The Office of the Mental Health Advocate supports public defenders, courts, and mental health facilities in navigating complex legal and clinical issues related to mental illness. Our specialized team brings expertise in both legal defense and mental health systems.

Our Expertise

We represent defendants with mental health challenges and handle post-adjudication insanity cases, ensuring that individuals receive appropriate legal representation that accounts for their unique circumstances and needs.

OUR SERVICES

What We Provide

Comprehensive mental health defense services for clients across Georgia.

Direct Representation

Advocates for clients acquitted by Reason of Insanity and represents limited Incompetent to Stand Trial (Non-Restorable) defendants.

Monitoring Mental Health Cases

Oversees Not Guilty by Reason of Insanity (NGRI) verdicts for consistency and legal representation in ongoing cases.

Informed Advocacy

Delivers specialized knowledge through collaboration and system-wide improvement initiatives to better serve clients with mental health needs.
