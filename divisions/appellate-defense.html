ABOUT THE DIVISION

Defending Rights Through Appeals

Our Mission

The Appellate Defense Division is dedicated to providing exceptional legal representation to indigent clients in their appeals before Georgia's highest courts. We ensure that constitutional rights are protected and that every client receives vigorous advocacy throughout the appellate process.

Our Expertise

Our team of experienced appellate attorneys specializes in complex legal analysis, persuasive brief writing, and compelling oral advocacy. We handle appeals arising from criminal convictions across our 45 circuit offices, bringing deep expertise to every case we handle.
