OUR MISSION

Defending Rights, Empowering Clients

The Georgia Public Defender Council (GPDC) is committed to providing high-quality legal defense for individuals accused of crimes who cannot afford representation. We believe that every person deserves a fair trial and competent legal counsel, regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our mission is to ensure that the constitutional right to counsel is upheld throughout the state of Georgia, protecting the rights of the accused and maintaining the integrity of our justice system.

OUR PURPOSE

Four Foundational Principles

Effective

We employ competent legal strategies and thorough case preparation to provide the most effective defense for every client.

Ethical

We maintain the highest standards of integrity, honoring our professional obligations and the trust placed in us by our clients.

Timely

We respond promptly to our clients' needs, understanding that swift action is often critical in legal matters.

Zealous

We advocate passionately for our clients, fighting tirelessly to protect their rights and achieve the best possible outcomes.
