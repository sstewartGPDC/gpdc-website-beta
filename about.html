OUR MISSION

Defending Rights, Empowering Clients

The Georgia Public Defender Council (GPDC) is committed to providing high-quality legal defense for individuals accused of crimes who cannot afford representation. We believe that every person deserves a fair trial and competent legal counsel, regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our mission is to ensure that the constitutional right to counsel is upheld throughout the state of Georgia, protecting the rights of the accused and maintaining the integrity of our justice system.

ORGANIZATIONAL INDEPENDENCE

An Independent and Autonomous Agency

The Georgia Public Defender Council operates as an independent agency within the state government's executive branch. This independence is essential to our mission, ensuring that our legal representation is free from political influence or private agendas.

Our autonomy allows us to focus solely on what matters most: providing the best possible defense for our clients and upholding the constitutional right to counsel.
