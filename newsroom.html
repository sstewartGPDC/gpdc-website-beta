Key Milestones

2020Pandemic Stability

Zero furloughs. Zero hiring freezes. New leadership steered GPDC through COVID-19 court shutdowns while protecting every staff position.

2021Federal Investment

Secured ARPA funding to clear pandemic case backlogs and expand capacity statewide.

2022Pay Equity

HB 1391 established compensation parity between Circuit Public Defenders and District Attorneys — a first in Georgia history.

2023–24Leadership Pipeline

Launched annual conferences, the Certified Public Defender Manager Program, and partnerships with UGA Law and the Carl Vinson Institute.

2025Expansion & Reform

New regional offices opened, largest-ever all-agency conference held, and a proposed APD-ADA common pay scale advanced for legislative review.
