## Position Overview

The GPDC Social Services Division is hiring a Social Worker to serve clients in the Stone Mountain Circuit. This role involves conducting biopsychosocial assessments and connecting clients with community resources.

## Requirements

- Master's degree in Social Work (MSW) preferred
- LMSW or LCSW licensure preferred
- Experience in criminal justice or forensic social work
- Cultural competency and trauma-informed practice skills
