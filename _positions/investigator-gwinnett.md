## Position Overview

The Gwinnett Circuit Public Defender's Office seeks an experienced Investigator to support our defense team by conducting thorough investigations in criminal cases.

## Requirements

- Prior law enforcement or investigative experience
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple cases
- Valid Georgia driver's license
